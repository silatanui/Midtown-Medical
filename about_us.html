About Us

Mid-Town Reproductive Health and Youth Friendly is a Level 2 Medical Clinic regulated by Nursing Council of Kenya (Private Practice) in Biashara ward, Nakuru County. Our facility was awarded for being one of the best Reproductive health Champion facilty in the country It opened its door in 2011

Our Mission

To be a center of excellence for quality healthcare provisions to all


Our Vision

To provide accessible, affordable and reliable quality healthcare

We Focus on

Informed Choice; Compassionate Care; Safe Services; Privacy and Confidentiality; Dignity and Comfort; Community Support;

The Founder

Rose Akinyi Otieno is a Community Health Nurse,mother, wife and grandmother. Born and raised in Nakuru county but originally from Siaya county. Currently in private practice running Midtown Reproductive Health &Youth Friendly clinic within Nakuru town Nakuru County.


My journey as a nurse started way back when I was in school. In form two if not for the intervention of my father who was Nakuru against me leaving school in form 2 I would have found myself in south Africa training as a nurse under a sponsored program.


After completing my fourth form I joined medical training school and trained as Kenya enrolled community health nurse,with my best subject being midwifery that earned me an award as the best theoretical Nurse in my January 1982 class incompletion.


After my college I was posted in siaya district hospital and has fate may have have it I worked more in Maternity unit and maternal child health clinic.
